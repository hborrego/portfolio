Thorncliffe Community Market

Made At:

ERA Architects

Role:

Product Researcher and Designer

When:

2016

Project Details:

Located in central east Toronto, Thorncliffe Park is densly populated neighborhood, home of a wide range of cultures and newcomers to Canada. Our team worked with with the Thorncliffe Park Action Group (TAG) as active participants of the Thorncliffe Community Market to design a series of market stands to be used during the event.

A group of users were involved in the design process from the beggining, which allowed us to prototype, test ideas and prioritize the needs that the TAG group were looking for

Our intend was to create the structural skeleton of the stand, which by design resulted in a minimal approach, allowing the users to become actve participants by giving the stands their own character

The users found a way to take ownership of the skeleton through the use of colorful fabrics — a very familiar and confortable elements for them

By allowing the users to finish the product, they were able to take ownership of their design approach and the food stand was a success
