Project Overview

I worked in the research and design for LeiaFlix — an App that allows users to rent and stream 3D Movies on the Lume Pad. The Lume Pad is Leia’s flagship tablet, which is capable of displaying 3D content without the need of goggles or glasses. The Lume Pad uses proprietary Light Field display technology which allows the tablet to showcase 3D as if it was coming out of the screen.

My role as lead designer consisted on conducting a Competitive Analysis of similar services, directing the Information Architecture, which informed the sitemap and flow diagrams, creating wireframes and testing them with users for validation, handing off high fidelity prototypes and documentation, and assisting with the Design QA process. screen.
