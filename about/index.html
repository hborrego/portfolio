I'm Herman Borrego, a product designer who specializes in digital interactions, spatial experiences and user research.

Trained as an architect and a designer, I thrive to be an active participant in projects that promote innovative solutions for our everyday devices and spaces. I am a believer that technology and space-making are allies and that by embracing emerging technologies, such as XR, we have the ability to enhance our physical space through digital solutions that generate memorable experiences.

My design process relies on contextual research, prototyping and user testing at its core. I'm inspired by peer feedback, iterative processes and unexpected findings. I'm committed to work closely with clients and users from diverse backgrounds who empower different perspectives and ideas.
