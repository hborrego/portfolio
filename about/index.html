About

I'm a Colombian-Canadian designer, currently residing in San Francisco, California, and working as a senior designer at Leia Inc. I hold a Bachelor of Design from OCAD University and a Master of Architecture from the University of Toronto, where I graduated with Honors.

My work focuses on designing user-centered and visually compelling products that empower people to accomplish more with fewer distractions. I thrive in projects with a business strategy in mind that promote innovative and memorable solutions for our everyday devices and lives.

My design process relies on contextual research, prototyping and user testing at its core. I am inspired by peer feedback, iterative processes, and unexpected findings. I am committed to working closely with clients and users from diverse backgrounds who embrace and promote different perspectives and ideas.

When I'm not designing, you probably can find me exploring the Bay Area on my bicycle.
