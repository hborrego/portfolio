I aim to create products and experiences that positively impact people and our environment. Trained as an architect and a designer, with extensive experience in multidisciplinary practices, I thrive to be an active participant in projects that promote innovative solutions in our everyday spaces and cities. I am a believer that technology and space-making are allies and that by embracing innovations, such as VR/AR/MR, we have the ability to efficiently test ideas, bridging the gap between the digital and the physical, and alleviate wasteful practices.

My design process relies on contextual research, prototyping and user testing at its core. I'm inspired by peer feedback, iterative processes and unexpected findings. I'm committed to work closely with clients and users from diverse backgrounds who empower different perspectives and ideas. When I make a mistake, I own it, embrace it and learn from it to better myself everyday, because failure and vulnerability are also aspects that make us human. Understanding both, the positive and negative, creates a unique opportunity, in which advantages and constraints become the catalysts of ideas and lessons.
