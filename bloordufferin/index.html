Bloor & Dufferin Development

Made At:

Giannone Petricone Associates

Role:

Designer: 3D Modeler and Visualization Strategist

When:

2018-2019

Project Details:

I made part of the team that brought to light the design intent and visuals for the Bloor & Dufferin development proposal in Toronto, Canada. We were responsible of modeling and create visual representations of street level experiences of this new mixed-use community.

We Created a Community Where Pedestrian Experiences Come First

Designs Inspired by the Community Feedback

The proposal evolved and continued to changed based on feedback received from the different stakeholders, including community neighbors and potential new residents. For instance, it was essencial to create social incobator spaces, such as a community hub, to build neighborhood driven initiatives, support systems, and activities.

All images displayed in this project are property of the Bloor Dufferin Partnership Ltd., led by Capital Developments and Metropia.
